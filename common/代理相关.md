## centos


1.查看当前代理

env | grep -i proxy

2.设置代理

export http_proxy=http://192.168.1.60:7890
export https_proxy=http://192.168.1.60:7890

3.取消代理
unset https_proxy
unset http_proxy

4.测试

curl http://www.google.com
